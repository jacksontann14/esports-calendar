How subscriptions work

Setup

  1. iPhone / Apple Calendar: tap the Apple button — it opens Calendar and prompts you to confirm.
  2. Google Calendar: tap the Google button — it opens calendar.google.com with an "Add this calendar?" prompt.
  3. Outlook: tap the Outlook button — it opens Outlook on the web to subscribe from URL directly.
  4. Anything else (Thunderbird, other apps): tap Copy link and paste it into that app's "subscribe from URL" option.
  5. Feeds refresh once a day automatically — your calendar app re-checks them on its own schedule (Google/Outlook can take up to 24 hours to pick up changes; Apple is usually faster).
